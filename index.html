Postdoctoral Researcher

Kyungpook National University, South Korea

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher specializing in advancing deep learning through neural network optimization, model compression, and computer vision applications. My research focuses on developing efficient AI systems using evolutionary algorithms and contributing to the scientific community through high-impact publications.

With expertise in multi/many-objective evolutionary algorithms, ensemble optimization methods, and neural network pruning, I work on solving complex real-world problems in areas such as federated learning, energy systems, and industrial IoT applications.
